What is this?

NAME

dangerously-skip-permissions — a collective of friends who let AI cook

SYNOPSIS

claude --dangerously-skip-permissions "build something unhinged"

DESCRIPTION

We're a group of friends who discovered that if you let Claude loose with --dangerously-skip-permissions, magical things happen. Not "startup magical." Not "disruptive magical." More like "why does this exist and why can't I stop using it" magical.

Every project on this site was born from a late-night prompt, a terrible idea, and the radical belief that software doesn't need a business model to deserve existing.

OPTIONS

--fun Required. If it's not fun, we're not building it.

--vibe-coded Every line was generated through the ancient art of vibing with AI.

--no-vc-funding This will never be a startup. You're welcome.

--friends Built by friends, for the internet, because we can.

AUTHORS

A revolving cast of humans with too much free time and one very patient AI.
